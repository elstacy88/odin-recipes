Alfredo Recipe

Creamy chicken alfredo served over Fettucini noodles

This simple chicken alfredo recipe is sure to be a new family favorite. Estimated cooking and prep time for this recipe is 30 minutes for a quick and easy meal. Add if your favorite vegetables or some bacon bites for customizable extras.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. Chicken
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped finely
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 qt heavy whipping cream
  • fresh or dried basil
  • salt and pepper
  • parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in sauce pan. Cut chicken into small bite-sized pieces and add to pan. Cook entirely and set aside in a small bowl.
  2. In the meantime, boil a pot of water and add fettucini noodles to cool about 8-10 minutes until al dente.
  3. In same pan, add more olive oil if needed. Add onion and cook until translucent.
  4. Add garlic and stir for 1 minute, careful not to burn.
  5. Melt butter in pan and lower heat.
  6. Add broccoli or cooked bacon at this point, or other vegetables, if desired.
  7. Add heavy cream and stir occasionally, bringing to a slow boil for about 5 minutes. It will get bubble and thicken into a sauce consistency.
  8. Add fresh or dried basil, mixed italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and other seasonings to taste.
  9. Add about 2 cups of grated parmesan cheese, spring for the more expensive kind it is worth it but any type will do.
  10. Mix until completely melted and sauce is thickened.
  11. Add al dente fettucini noodles to pan with a couple cups of pasta water and cover with lid.
  12. Simmer about 5 minutes to cook pasta the rest of the way, and voila!
  13. Top with more parmesan cheese, and serve with toasted garlic bread.
